Services

A major restaurant chain improves efficiency by inclusion of disabled workers

We helped a major restaurant adapt their processes to accommodate visually impaired team members. The result was overall increase in efficiency and a sense of contributing to society from the stores and executive management.

A factory engages with the community and gives their leadership team a new sense of purpose

We helped a factory develop a recruiting program that included individuals that had experienced homelessness in their local community. The result was reciprocal goodwill between management and the community, and a new dimension of purpose among the leadership team.

A small business designed around a powerful mission

We helped a small coffee shop develop a social mission from the ground up. The disadvantaged migrant workers they initially hired as bicycle delivery drivers and entry-level workers are now managing new stores.
