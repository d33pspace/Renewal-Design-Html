When temperatures drop, people urgently need warm clothes, coats and hats.

Renewal’s drop-in center is offering warm clothes, food and kindness to lonely homeless people who will be struggling to survive on the cold streets at night. We are urgently requesting gifts from supporters – because the cost of food is rising and the first people want when they come to us is a good, hot meal.

Read the story here

Our autumn newsletter also tells the survival story of Jiang Yuanyan, who found his way to the Renewal Center during lockdown, despite the drop-in center being closed for most of the last seven months. It was wonderful to see Jiang Yuanyan start a new job – and to know that it would have been impossible without the training provided here, and the kind gifts of generous supporters like you.
